Who We Are:
Dynamus Unified Tech Solutions (UTS) is a premier consulting firm specializing in Information Technology Solutions and Services for Federal, State, Local governments and commercial clients.  We partner with organizations by developing strategies that integrates customized IT solutions and services leveraging cutting-edge technologies from both the public and private sectors to make critical business decisions that support organizational goals and objectives.  With over 50 years of combined experience in both the public and private sectors delivering high-quality IT solutions and services, our team guarantees exceptionally performance in all aspects of the acquisition process while meeting or exceeding expectations throughout the entire value chain from customers to all our team members. 

The Dynamus Experience:
Our extensive experience in both the public and private sectors in Information Technology, Information/ Cyber Security, and Program Management enables our team to deliver tangible business results for our clients in achieving and realizing substantial return on investment from the strategic use of technology.  We strive to meet business requirements and build long-term relationships based on mutual trust and respect.  Dynamus Unified Tech Solutions is committed to excellence and creating innovative and flexible solutions with all our Partners.

Veterans First Commitment:
Being Veterans ourselves, we understand the challenges facing Veterans and Service Members with transitioning and translating skills forged under fire in training and real-world experiences while serving our country.  The integrity, teamwork, perseverance, and adaptability that is instilled and demonstrated by all Veterans and Service Members on a daily basis are all critical attributes that all employers are ultimately seeking and is why Dynamus Unified Tech Solutions is committed in ensuring that Veterans and Service Members have first priority when fulfilling business and partner requirements.
